Household Waste Awareness



Learn what household waste is

Household waste can be any waste that comes from our household. These includes:

  • househould rubbish
  • unwanted or unused items such as old furniture, electrical items and etc.,
  • garden waste

Why do the household waste issue matters?

There were some disorders and injuries among the household waste collector around worldwide, such as respiratory problems, infectious diseases, muscle pain, fever, headache and etc.,
Throwing away household waste improperly can cause pollution, health hazard and contribute to climate change. Despite this, it's an area that is often overlooked or misunderstood.
